Company "ALEX ENGINEERING" has been successfully operating in the telecommunications market since 2002 and is staffed with highly qualified specialists in the fields of telecommunications and the application of alternative energy sources. Over 23 years in the telecommunications and energy sectors, our company has accumulated extensive experience working with various organizations across the Republic of Uzbekistan.

We perform pre-project studies, design, construction, installation and commissioning works, as well as turnkey construction of base stations for mobile operators — with participation in a broad portfolio of national telecom and energy projects.

At present, our company is actively engaged in the solar energy sector, providing turnkey solar power plant (EPC: Engineering, Procurement, Construction) services. Our specialists have experience implementing solar PV projects under the climatic conditions of Central Asia. Our main goal: to create reliable, efficient and economically viable solar power generation systems.

OUR JOURNEY · SINCE 2002

From telecom to solar

Two decades of telecommunications engineering, now channeled into turnkey solar EPC.

  1. 2002

    Company founded — start in the telecommunications market.

  2. 2000s

    Contract works for Huawei (GSM, CDMA, WCDMA, WiMAX, SDH, DWDM); commissioning for Unitel, Uzdunrobita, Coscom, Perfectum, Uzmobile.

  3. 2000s–2010s

    Uzbektelecom network development; SDH/OSN commissioning; antenna-mast structures nationwide.

  4. 2010s

    Diesel generators for mobile operators; security & fire alarm systems.

  5. Recent years

    Shift to solar energy — turnkey solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction).

  6. Flagship

    30 MW PV plant with solar tracking, Samarkand — subcontractor of "China Energie" L.T.D.

  7. 2025

    8+ PV projects delivered across Uzbekistan; ~38 MW installed.
